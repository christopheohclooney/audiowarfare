About

Audio Warfare Studios is a recording and mixing studio built inside the UK hardcore scene, not adjacent to it.

Darius and Christy have spent decades in bands, on stages, and in the crowd. The studio grew from that — a space where bands don't have to explain what they're going for, because we already know.

We record loud, fast, and honest. We mix for weight, clarity, and impact. Every record we make is made by people who care about it.

The walls are covered in thirty years of UKHC flyers. That's not decoration. That's the point.
